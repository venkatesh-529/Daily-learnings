- CASE inside SUM is a powerful pattern for classification counts

- Aggregating first and filtering later leads to cleaner logic

## Mistake I Made

Extracting year and month fixed the aggregation level.

## Queries to Remember

### Odd and Even Transactions
```sql
SELECT
    transaction_date,
    SUM(CASE WHEN amount % 2 = 1 THEN amount ELSE 0 END) AS odd_sum,
    SUM(CASE WHEN amount % 2 = 0 THEN amount ELSE 0 END) AS even_sum
FROM Transactions
GROUP BY transaction_date;
